About Country Place

A small, friendly community where neighbors look out for each other.

Our Story

Country Place has been a part of the Hico community for years. What started as a small mobile home park has grown into a mixed-use residential community offering furnished cabins, RV pads, and mobile home sites — all on one quiet, well-maintained property.

We have an on-site point of contact who lives right here in the community, so there's always someone nearby when you need help. We take pride in keeping things simple and straightforward — no hidden fees, just honest, affordable housing with personal attention and a hands-on approach.

What Makes Us Different

Furnished & Move-In Ready

Our cabins come furnished, so you can move in without the hassle and expense of buying furniture. Bring your clothes, your dog, and your morning coffee — we've got the rest covered.

Utilities Included

Rent at Country Place includes water, electricity, and trash service. One payment, no surprise bills. You'll know exactly what you're spending each month.

Pet-Friendly Living

We know your pets are family. Country Place welcomes pets, so you don't have to choose between affordable housing and keeping your companion by your side.

Month-to-Month Flexibility

We rent on a month-to-month basis, so you're never locked into a long-term lease. That said, most of our residents choose to stay — and we think that says a lot about the community.

The Hico Lifestyle

Hico, Texas is one of those small towns that people stumble into and never want to leave. With a charming downtown, friendly locals, and some of the prettiest countryside in the state, it's an ideal place to slow down and enjoy life.

The town sits at the northern edge of the Texas Hill Country along the Bosque River. It's big enough to have what you need — groceries, hardware, dining — and small enough that people still wave when they drive by.

If you work in Stephenville, Glen Rose, Meridian, or anywhere in the Hamilton County area, Country Place puts you in a central location with an easy commute and a peaceful place to come home to.
